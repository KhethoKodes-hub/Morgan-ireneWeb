About Us

Morgan & Irene Corp is a 100% Black-owned company registered in 2023 in South Africa (Company No: 2023/172382/07). We specialize in various construction and engineering services, priding ourselves on quality provision that reflects the character and reputation of our company.

With a commitment to excellence and a focus on delivering exceptional value, we approach every project with the same dedication and attention to detail that has become our trademark.

To provide great business values and optimized solutions needed by our clients to finally improve our stakeholders' quality of life.

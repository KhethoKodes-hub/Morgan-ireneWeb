Our Story

Morgan & Irene Corp is a 100% Black-owned company registered in 2023 in South Africa (Company No: 2023/172382/07). Founded with a vision to transform the construction industry through excellence, integrity, and innovation, we have quickly established ourselves as a trusted partner for construction, civil engineering, and maintenance services.

Our name represents the coming together of diverse expertise and shared values. We believe that great construction is not just about buildings and infrastructure, but about building relationships, communities, and futures.

Our Vision

To provide great business values and optimized solutions needed by our clients to finally improve our stakeholders' quality of life.

Our Mission

To procure projects at competitive pricing, provide safe working conditions and deliver quality work within reasonable time frame.

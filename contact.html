Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

How quickly can you start a project after consultation?

We typically begin projects within 1-2 weeks after the initial consultation, depending on project complexity and material availability. For urgent projects, we can expedite the process.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide detailed, free estimates for all projects after an initial consultation. Our estimates include a breakdown of materials, labor, and timeline.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Gauteng region, including Johannesburg, Pretoria, and surrounding areas. For larger projects, we may consider work in other provinces.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Morgan & Irene Corp is fully licensed (Company No: 2023/172382/07) and carries comprehensive insurance for all our projects to protect both our team and your property.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

We offer a 12-month warranty on all our workmanship. Specific warranties for materials may vary by supplier and will be detailed in your project contract.
